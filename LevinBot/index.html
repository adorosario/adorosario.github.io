About LevinBot

LevinBot was developed by Rui Do Rosario using CustomGPT, a ChatGPT-powered platform that lets businesses build chatbots with their own data. It has been trained on research papers and transcripts of talks from the Levin Lab, including most past papers (PDFs) and audio/video transcripts.
